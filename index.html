Course Offers

BSINFO

The BSINFO program at LSPU College of Computer Studies is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in the rapidly evolving field of information technology. Through a combination of theoretical learning and hands-on experience, students will learn about software development, database management, networking, and cybersecurity. Graduates of this program are prepared for careers as IT professionals in various industries.

BSCS

The BSCS program at LSPU College of Computer Studies provides students with a comprehensive education in computer science principles and practices. Students will learn about algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and software engineering. This program emphasizes problem-solving skills and prepares graduates for careers in software development, computer systems analysis, and other related fields.
